Chismix

Chismix

Graphic Design/Interactive Media

As a personal commitment to making learning accessible, I thought of designing a language learning app that focuses on what I believe is the most crucial part of language use for most people– the ability to use it in conversations. 

Chismix is a mobile app concept that aims to provide interested language learners a platform to practice mainly how to speak with and listen to their target language.
